About the Gator Amateur Radio Club

The club was founded initially in 1934, and has evolved over it's 90+ years. From a world war to a global pandemic, UF hams have remained radioactive.

For more information about the club history, see the archives curated by the 2009 GARC group in honor of the 75th anniversary of the club (Link to archives)
